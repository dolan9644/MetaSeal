## 🌐 English

**MetaSeal** is a minimalist desktop app for artists, writers, and developers. It helps you protect your digital work from being stolen or used by AI without your permission. Everything runs 100% on your computer—no cloud, no data leaks.

### ✨ What can it do?
1. **Photo Protection**: 
   - **Hidden Watermark**: Add "invisible" signatures to your photos. Even if someone takes a screenshot or compresses the image, your info stays there.
   - **Anti-AI Shield**: Add subtle noise to your art that confuses AI models, preventing them from "copying" your style.
2. **Text Proof**: Inject invisible characters into your articles or notes to track and prove you are the original author.
3. **Code Safety**: Automatically add secure, unchangeable signatures to your source code.
4. **Blockchain Proof**: One-click to save a digital "fingerprint" of your work to the Arweave/Bitcoin network as permanent legal proof.

### 🚀 Why MetaSeal?
- **Total Privacy**: Your files never leave your machine.
- **Easy to Use**: Drag and drop your work, click "Protect", and you're done.
- **Apple-Style UI**: A clean, distraction-free interface built for creators.

## 🇨🇳 简体中文

**MetaSeal (元印)** 是一款简单的工具，帮您给自己的作品（图片、文字、代码）加上“防盗锁”。它采用本地运行模式，不联网、不泄露隐私。

### ✨ 它能帮你做什么？
1. **图片版权保护**: 
   - **隐藏水印**: 在照片里嵌入肉眼看不见的署名。即使别人截图、压缩或裁剪，你依然可以证明这是你的作品。
   - **防 AI 抓取**: 给画作添加特殊的“噪音”，干扰 AI 模型的训练，防止你的画风被 AI 无端模仿。
2. **文字确权**: 在文章或笔记中植入隐藏字符。即使别人复制粘贴到其他地方，你依然能追踪并证明原创。
3. **代码安全**: 为源码自动添加不可改写的数字签名。
4. **区块链存证**: 一键连入 Arweave 和比特币网络，为作品打上永久的时间戳，提供法律级的初创证据。

### 🚀 为什么选择元印？
- **绝对隐私**: 100% 本地运算，文件绝不上传云端，不离机。
- **极简操作**: 拖入文件，点击“开始保护”，剩下的交给软件。
- **纯粹设计**: 专为创作者设计的极简界面，无冗余，无干扰。
